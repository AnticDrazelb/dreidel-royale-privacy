Dreidel Royale — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 20, 2026

Dreidel Royale is a game. It has no accounts, no analytics, no advertising, and no interest in your data. This page explains the little that does happen.

Data we collect

None. Dreidel Royale does not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information. There is no sign-up, no server operated by us, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Game progress — statistics, unlocked dreidel skins, and settings — is saved locally on your device only. Uninstalling the app deletes it. It is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Camera

The optional "Play on your table" mode uses your camera so the game can be displayed over your real surroundings. The camera image is rendered on your screen in real time and nothing more: it is never recorded, stored, or transmitted. The camera is only active while that mode is open, and the app works fully without ever granting camera access.

Online multiplayer

Online games connect players directly to each other (peer-to-peer WebRTC). The nickname you type and live gameplay state (spins, scores) are sent to the players in your room and to no one else. A third-party signalling service (PeerJS) is used briefly to establish the connection between peers; it does not receive gameplay content. Room codes are temporary and expire when the room closes.

Third-party content

The app may load fonts from Google Fonts, which involves a standard web request to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy applies to that request. The game functions without it.

Children

The app is directed at a general audience. It contains no ads, no purchases, no chat beyond preset game messages, and collects no data from anyone, including children.
